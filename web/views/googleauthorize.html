Fetch Fetch your Office 365 Calendar
    Account Linking

Fetch reads your Office 365 calendar events. Just say "Ok Google, ask fetch my calendar what is happening today?". Fetch will read the subjects and time of your upcoming events.

Please sign in to you Office 365 account to give Fetch permissions to access your Calendar.
